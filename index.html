Key Projects & Initiatives

Digital Survey Transformation

Identified low engagement with paper-based staff surveys, which hindered management's ability to gather actionable feedback. The process was slow, and anonymity concerns led to a lack of genuine responses.

My Solution:

I spearheaded the transition to a digital survey system using Microsoft Forms. This involved designing the survey, ensuring anonymity, and creating a communication plan to encourage participation. I then analyzed the collected data to present key findings and recommendations to the management team.

Impact:

  • 50% increase in staff response rates.
  • 99% genuine feedback captured, leading to data-driven action plans.
  • Streamlined the feedback loop from weeks to days.

No-Code Task Allocation App

Daily task delegation was managed through paper lists and verbal instructions, leading to a lack of accountability, inconsistent completion of duties, and no real-time visibility for shift leaders.

My Solution:

Leveraging my interest in AI and process automation, I designed and built a no-code task allocation application using Glide. The app provided a simple, mobile-first interface for staff to view their assigned tasks and mark them as complete.

Impact:

  • Significantly improved staff accountability and task tracking.
  • Provided management with real-time performance monitoring.
  • Eliminated paper processes, reducing administrative overhead.
